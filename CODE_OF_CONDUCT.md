# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate. Contributors and maintainers are expected to:

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and accept constructive technical feedback.
- Keep discussion focused on the work and its impact.
- Avoid harassment, threats, insults, discriminatory language, or unwanted attention.
- Protect private information, credentials, customer data, and Slack workspace data.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and other project-maintained communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this policy. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the project and its community.

If you need to report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository's public maintainer channel and ask for an appropriate private reporting path when the details should not be public. Do not include private personal information, credentials, or security-sensitive details in a public report.
